ABOUT

I'm Pepper Curry. I'm a designer across a variety of platforms, including web, print, branding, and illustration. I specialise in web and branding.

I recieved a BFA in Fine Art from the prestigious California Institute of the Arts in 2011. Since then, I relocated to Wellington, New Zealand. I've worked for a variety of creative businesses here, including BizDojo, The Jig Lab, and currently, Rabid.

What makes me a unique designer is that I bring a fine art sensibility to user experience, designing flows that work together beautifully for the user, but also have a certain flair to them. This means that each design I create is bespoke, constructed with your needs in mind, and assembled with care.

I'm also an avid mountain biker, knitter, and Magic: The Gathering player. I'm enthusiastic about projects that have positive social impact, especially in the areas of environmentalism, social equality, and education.
