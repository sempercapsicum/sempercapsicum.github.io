ABOUT

I'm Pepper Curry, a web designer and illustrator with over three years of freelance and dev shop experience. I live in Wellington, New Zealand, a vibrant city with a strong technological and creative culture. Since graduating with a BFA from the California Institute of Arts in 2011, I've been doing design work for an varied range of clients (scientists, 3D technologists, government offices, environmentalists, etc.). I moved to New Zealand, and gained a position as the sole Designer at Rabid, a dev shop who focuses on projects for social good. Here, I've honed my skills as a web designer with strong responsive and UX skills.

My work is recognised for being minimal, clean, and functional. I bring colour and movement to web principles that work, instead of trying to reinvent the wheel. While this means that my work may not be lone-wolf revolutionary, it means I communicate better with development teams, clients, and users. My wild side tends to come through in my illustrations, which come from a strong Fine Arts background at CalArts.

My current life goal is to move out to Northland, NZ with my partner, Peter, who is a professional chef. We want to have a small homestead to offset our busy careers. I'm enthusiastic about finding full-time, remote work that enables me to live where I like, while still remaining connected to the web community.

Some of my other interests include gardening, knitting, tabletop/desktop gaming, mountain biking, and VJ/DJing. I'm a secret nerd who plays Magic: The Gathering, but can also be found at house gigs in Wellington with my audio-reactive visuals.
