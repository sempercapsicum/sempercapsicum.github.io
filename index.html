Pepper Curry is a designer and illustrator currently working in Wellington, New Zealand, with an eye towards transitioning to remote work. She's been working in New Zealand for three years.

Pepper has designed solutions for organisations such as the New Zealand Privacy Commission, NZ Navigator, Retail NZ, Grow Wellington/Wellington City Council, Papa Taiao - Earthcare, PledgeMe, and many more (see work section for highlights). She's skilled at working directly with developers, and has strengths in UX, UI, and responsive design.

Beginning with workshopping, to prototyping and wireframing, to styling and branding, Pepper is used to seeing a project through to the finish. She'll work closely with development to ensure that the design's focus remains on user experience, consistency, and aesthetic simplicity. She excels at creating natural, minimal user flows.

Pepper's interests outside of work include yoga, VJ/DJing, gardening, knitting, tabletop/digital gaming, and illustration. Her plans for the future are to find a remote working solution that enables her to live in the remote reaches of Northland, New Zealand with her partner, Peter.
